FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Does datasafe work in all devices?

Our Backup Manager is available for Windows, OSx and Linux devices

What happens to my data if I unsubscribe from the service?

All of your data will be deleted at the end of your subscription.

Can I do my backups locally?

Yes, you can define a local path where you will keep an exact copy of what you have in the cloud.

Can I deactivate the Cloud backups?

No, the cloud backup is the main backup.

For how long do you keep my backups?

Initially you data will be kept for 5 years. If you need more time for legal reason, please contact us.

Can I increase the time my backups are saved?

Yes, please contact us with your needs.

Can I increase the size of my backups?

Yes, at any time.

Can I put a GB limit so I do not spend extra?

Yes, you can limit the amount of GB by the predefined amounts (100-200-300-400-500..)

Is the software available in english or other languages?

You can change the language after downloading our Datasafe backup manager.

How often will I pay?

Monthly basis.

Can I get an invoice?

Yes, simply add the company details through your personal dashboard and an invoice will be generated at the end of each billing cycle.
